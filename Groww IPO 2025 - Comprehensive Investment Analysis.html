Q2: What is the price band?

Q3: Should I apply for the Groww IPO?

A: Short-term: Cautious optimism - premium valuation but strong fundamentals. Expect moderate listing gains (10-20%). Long-term: Positive view for 3-5 year investors believing in India's fintech story. Consider your risk appetite, investment horizon, and portfolio allocation before applying.

Retail allocation: Minimum lot size will be announced, typically ‚Çπ10,000-15,000 for retail investors.

Q6: What is the lot size and minimum investment?

A: Lot size will be announced in the RHP. Retail investors typically need to apply for minimum 1 lot. Maximum retail application is typically ‚Çπ2 lakhs to qualify for retail quota benefits.

Q7: Why is Groww trading at premium to peers?

A: Justified by: (1) Superior margins - 45% vs peers 23-35%, (2) Market leadership - 26.3% share, (3) Higher growth - 59% CAGR vs peers 26-47%, (4) Quality metrics - organic growth, retention, engagement all best-in-class.

Q8: What happens to my Groww account if I invest in the IPO?

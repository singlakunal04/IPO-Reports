E2E Transportation Infrastructure Limited is a system integrator and engineering solutions provider specializing in end-to-end railway infrastructure projects. The company provides integrated solutions covering:

Signalling & Telecom

CBTC signalling for metro networks, electronic interlocking upgrades, S&T modernization

Track Electrification

Overhead Equipment (OHE) installation, track electrification projects across mainline and metro

Turnkey Solutions

End-to-end design, procurement, installation, testing for civil and track components

About Park Medi World

Company Profile

  • Founded: January 20, 2011
  • Headquarters: North India (Haryana, Delhi, Punjab, Rajasthan)
  • Position: 2nd largest private hospital chain in North India
  • Bed Capacity: 3,000 beds (1,600 in Haryana - highest in the region)
  • Hospital Network: 14 NABH-accredited multi-super speciality hospitals
  • Lead Managers: Nuvama Wealth Management, CLSA India, DAM Capital Advisors, Intensive Fiscal Services
  • Registrar: Kfin Technologies Limited

Healthcare Services Offered

Park Medi World operates over 30 super-speciality and speciality services including:
  • Internal Medicine, Neurology, Urology, Gastroenterology
  • General Surgery, Orthopaedics, Oncology
  • Critical Care, Cardiology, Pediatrics, Emergency Services
  • Diagnostic Services, Laboratory & Radiology

Business Model Strength

Revenue Diversification: Government Schemes & PSUs contribute 83.38% of revenue, reducing cyclical risk during economic downturns. This stable income stream ensures predictable cash flows.

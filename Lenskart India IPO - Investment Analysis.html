Key Risks & Concerns

⚠ Premium Valuation Risk
Trading at 227x P/E ratio vs peers at 35-50x, leaving little room for error
⚠ Growth Deceleration
Revenue growth slowed from 43% (FY24) to 23% (FY25), margin expansion may plateau
⚠ Low ROE
Return on Equity at 4.84% vs industry norm of 12-15%, capital efficiency concerns
⚠ Execution Risk
Aggressive 620-store expansion plan requires flawless execution
⚠ Intensifying Competition
Titan Eye+ expanding, Reliance entering, international players eyeing India
⚠ Large OFS Component
70% of IPO is Offer for Sale (promoters/investors exiting), signaling caution
